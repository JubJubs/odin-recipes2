Ramen

Description

A simple and delicious spin on the typical ramen noodles that can be easily made!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 (3oz) package of any flavor ramen noodles
  • 1 slice American cheese

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan. Add ramen noodles and cook 2 minutes until tender. Pour out water, then stir in seasoning packet and cheese to serve.
